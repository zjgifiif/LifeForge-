## 🌟 What is LifeForge?

**LifeForge** is a comprehensive **Gamified Life Management System** built for Android. If you struggle with procrastination, ADHD, or simply finding the motivation to complete daily tasks, LifeForge is your ultimate companion. 

We transformed standard to-do lists into an immersive RPG (Role-Playing Game) experience. Stop putting things off and start earning Experience Points (EXP), unlocking achievements, and purchasing real-life rewards with the credits you earn!

## 🚀 Epic Features

* 📝 **The Quest System:** Treat your daily chores and professional tasks as quests. Classify them by difficulty (Easy, Normal, Hard) and earn proportional Coins and EXP.
* 🧠 **Real-Life Skill Trees:** Link tasks to specific skills (e.g., Coding, Fitness, Reading, Languages). Level up your competencies from Novice (Lv.1) to Grandmaster (Lv.100).
* 🛒 **Custom Reward Shop:** Use the in-game Coins you earn to buy real-life rewards you set for yourself (e.g., "1 Hour of Gaming," "Buy a Coffee"). Link effort directly to rewards.
* 🏆 **Achievement Hall:** Unlock beautifully designed badges and trophies for reaching milestones. Track your long-term success and build your legacy.
* 🎯 **Smart Goals Tracking:** Set measurable KPIs and objectives. The app automatically tracks your progress through smart algorithms.
* 👑 **Lifetime VIP Access:** No annoying monthly subscriptions! A highly secure, one-time crypto payment unlocks unlimited access forever.
* 🌙 **Dark Mode Ready:** Sleek, modern Material Design UI that adapts to your system preferences.

## 📥 Download & Install (v1.1)

LifeForge is incredibly lightweight (**only 19 MB**) and highly compatible, supporting a massive range of devices from **Android 5.0 (Lollipop)** all the way to the upcoming **Android 16**.

### 🟢 [Download Latest APK (v1.1) Here](../../releases/latest)

**Installation Steps:**
1. Click the download link above and download the `lifeforge_v1.1.apk` file from the Assets section.
2. Open the downloaded APK on your Android device.
3. If prompted, allow your browser/file manager to **"Install from Unknown Sources"**.
4. Launch the app, sign in securely via Google, and start your adventure!
